# Who Am I? Specification

## Overview
A storytelling page that presents your professional journey as a continuous narrative. Background flows into education, then experience, and ends with your current role — all readable top to bottom with clean typography and subtle fade-in animations on scroll.

## User Flows
- Visitor lands on the page and reads your story from top to bottom
- Content sections fade in smoothly as they scroll into view
- No clicks required — just scroll and read

## UI Requirements
- Vertical narrative layout with generous spacing
- Four content sections in order: Background, Education, Experience, Current Role
- Subtle fade-in animations as sections enter the viewport
- Clean typography (Inter), minimal styling
- Mobile responsive — reads well on all devices
