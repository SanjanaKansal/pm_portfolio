# Mutual Fit

## Overview
A 2-row grid showing what I bring to a role and what I look for in a workplace.

## User Flows
- View what I bring (4 items)
- View what I look for (4 items)

## UI Requirements
- 2-row grid layout with row labels on the left
- Each row has 4 cards
- Purple accent for "What I Bring"
- Green accent for "What I Look For"
